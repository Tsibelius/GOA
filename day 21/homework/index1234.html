Paragliding in Gonio

about gonio paragliding

Best time to visit: SUMMER

Are you resting in Adjara and looking for extreme sensations? Do you want to feel the same as the birds feel during the flight?
Paragliding is already possible in Gonio, just a 10-minute drive from Batumi.
Enjoy the beauty of the Black Sea and Adjara mountains. Feel the freedom and adrenaline rush!
Paragliding Club XCaucasus Paragliding offers you paragliding in Gonio. XCaucasus Paragliding is a club of certified pilots. The main priority of XCaucasus Paragliding is your maximum safety. All aircraft are properly equipped and constantly checked by our pilots. Immediately before the flight, the traveler is given a brief instruction on the features of take-off and landing.
Price includes:
Flight
Shooting with a GoPro camera
Recommendations
Sportswear and footwear are best for flying.
If you have any health problems, be sure to provide us with the relevant information.
Be sober.
